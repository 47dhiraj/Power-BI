
# DAX(Data Analysis Expressions):

==> DAX is a formula language used in Power BI to create custom calculations and expressions for data analysis and modeling. 

==> DAX functions are the building blocks of these calculations and can be used to perform a wide range of operations on your data. 


# How to write a DAX function ?

        0. At first, be on the Report view.
        1. Right click on "Table" in the Data section.
        2. And, Select "New Measure" option.
        3. And then, write the DAX function & also providing name to measure.
        4. Now, Hit Enter OR click on Tick Icon.
        5. Finally, Do Tick/check  this new measure created under the Table in Data section.

# How to "Remove Rows" ??

    
    1) Navigate to "Home" tab in the Ribbon.

    2) Click on "Remove Rows" icon.

    3) A dropdow option will appear. 

    4) U can choose any options from the below, like:

        * Remove Top Rows
        * Remove Bottom Rows
        * Remove Blank Rows
        * Remove Duplicates
        * Remove Errors
    
    5) A dialgo box will appear.

    6) Specify the "Number of rows" u want to select.

    7) Finally, click on "OK" button.
